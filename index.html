Lucky Charms Collector

How To Play

At the beginning of each game a random number is generated. By clicking on the four lucky charms, which are each associated to a number beetween 1 and 12, you add to your score. You win the game by matching your score to the random number. Note that the value of each charm will only be revealed after a click. Bonne chance!!!
